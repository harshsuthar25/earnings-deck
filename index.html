rev = np.array([1780, 1850, 1910, 1995, 2080, 2165, 2250, 2480])  # $M

Valuation & Returns

Net Present Value (NPV):

\[ \mathrm{NPV} = \sum_{t=1}^{T} \frac{CF_t}{(1+r)^t} - C_0 \]

Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC):

\[ \mathrm{WACC} = \frac{E}{D+E}k_e + \frac{D}{D+E}k_d(1-\tau) \]

Use NPV to frame long‑term investment decisions. Tie WACC to current rate environment.
